    ​Editing and proofreading

    We have a team of professional proof-readers who ensure all translations are free of spelling, grammatical and linguistic errors. Our Quality Assurance systems have proven effective in ensuring that our clients stay on our client list. We believe that the quality of our translations starts with the quality of our translators.     Transcription

    Offering fast and precise audio transcription services, converting the spoken language into a written form. Upon listening to your recording, we will appoint transcribers specializing in the required subject matter to type out word-for-word what is being spoken. We can also subsequently translate your transcript.

    Subtitling

    Providing subtitling and captioning services in an efficient way of making an audio or video universally applicable. Our linguistic experts effectively study and analyze every audio and visual input during the content creation process. They then create quality subtitles by replacing words in the original document with a synonym that expresses the same feeling. Thus, the translated subtitles which we offer communicate the original message without changing its meaning.
